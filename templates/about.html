About Themis

Making legislative information accessible to everyone

What is Themis?

Themis is a legislative alert system that helps people stay informed about bills and laws that directly affect their lives. Every year, thousands of bills pass through state and federal legislatures that impact civil rights, healthcare, education, employment, and more—but most people never hear about them until it's too late.

We built Themis to solve this problem. Our platform automatically tracks legislation based on your interests and sends you personalized notifications when relevant bills are introduced or passed, with summaries written in plain language.

Why This Matters

Legislative changes can have profound effects on people's daily lives, but tracking this information requires constant monitoring of multiple legislative databases, understanding complex legal language, and time that most people don't have.

Women, LGBTQ+ individuals, and other marginalized groups are often disproportionately affected by legislation, yet face barriers to civic engagement. Themis removes these barriers by bringing the information directly to you.

How It Works

  1. Select Your Interests: Choose the policy areas that matter to you—reproductive rights, healthcare, education, voting access, criminal justice, and more.
  2. We Track Legislation: Our system monitors state and federal legislative databases every 30 minutes, scraping new bills as they're introduced.
  3. AI Summarization: Complex legal text is processed through AI to generate plain-language summaries and extract relevant policy tags.
  4. Smart Matching: Bills are automatically matched to users based on their selected interests.
  5. Get Notified: You receive push notifications when legislation affecting you is introduced or passes, with links to the full text.

The Technology

  • Web scraping: Collects bills from Indiana's legislative database
  • AI/NLP: Summarizes bills and extracts policy categories
  • MongoDB: Stores bills and user preferences
  • Web push notifications: Real-time alerts
  • Flask backend: Automated scheduling checks for new legislation every 30 minutes

Why "Themis"?

In Greek mythology, Themis is the goddess of divine law, order, and justice. She's depicted holding scales, symbolizing fairness and balance. Themis had the gift of foresight and served as an advisor on matters of justice.

We chose this name because our platform helps you see what's coming in the legislative landscape and make informed decisions about laws that affect you—just like Themis guided the ancient Greeks toward fair and just outcomes.
